Training FAQ

From where can I access Learning?

A: You can access Learning from your Success Factors. Click the Home dropdown and click on the learning tab.

How can I view the calender and book courses?

A: You can view the training calendar from Find Learning in the Learning section, either you browse all courses or you can type the name of the course. Another option would to browse through the courses here on our website.

A: Who can give new RSTC role on LMS to schedule and update Rig in house training?

For RSTC to get a role in LMS to schedule classes and to be added as an instructor, he can send an email to TrainingAdmin@arabdrill.com for the training team to add him in LMS as an instructor and grant him the role. Once done, RSTC needs to follow the steps in the attached presentation on how to schedule and update training in LMS.

How can RSTC book Rig crew in LMS?

A: Once the RSTC given the access to view and plan the Rig crew booking, he can follow the steps showing in the attached presentation.

What information is needed to add 3rd party in LMS, and who can add it?

A: To add 3rd party to your location in LMS, you can send an email including the following information:

1. Iqama number
2. Name
3. Position

For AD, OFSAT employees and 3rd party who are holding a company badge number, who can change their location or add/remove them from the system?

A: For any user in LMS holding a company badge number, only the HR team can add or remove them from the system. The action needs to be completed on Success Factors, which will reflect within 24 hours in LMS.

How can Rig employees or OFSAT employees view their compliance

A: For AD Rig crew, their compliance is available in the AD Power Bi Matrix report, which can be access by clicking HERE or through Success Factors Dashboards.

For OFSAT crew their compliance is available in the OFSAT Power Bi Matrix report, which can be accessed by clicking HERE

What forms need to be completed to add a new course in LMS, or change the current assignment for a course?

A: To add a new course in LMS, you need to fill up Training Development Request and get all the required approvals. And to change the current assignment in the system to add or remove positions or locations, you need to fill up Training Amendment Request.

For general requests or clarifications regarding Training, who should I contact?

A: For any support related to training, please contact the Training Admin team at their shared email: Trainingadmin@arabdrill.com
